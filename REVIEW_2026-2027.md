## 4. Practical day assignments (AM/PM)

For practicals **already in the 2025-2026 P5 CSV**, I reused their known-good
`Days` values (e.g. `TueAM, FriPM`).

For practicals **only in P1 / P2 / P4** (or new this year), the frame schedule
gives only the day of the week (e.g. "Mon/Wed/Fri"), not AM/PM. I used the
conservative default: each listed day occupies both AM and PM. This means the
planner may flag **more conflicts than real** — a practical on "Mon/Wed/Fri"
will appear to conflict with any course that meets any time on Mon, Wed, or
Fri.

**Action:** once you know the exact AM/PM half-day for each practical, update
the `Days` column of the CSV. Format is `"DayAM, DayPM"` comma-separated (use
double quotes because it contains a comma).
